LORD MURUGAN

ABOUT HIM

Murugan, also known as Kartikeya, is a Hindu god of war, victory, and wisdom, primarily worshipped in South India and by Tamil communities worldwide. He is the son of Shiva and Parvati, known for defeating the demon Tarakasura and is depicted as a young man with a spear called the Vel and a peacock as his mount. His story involves his birth from the combined energies of Shiva and Parvati, with his six heads representing the six siddhis (powers) bestowed upon yogis.
